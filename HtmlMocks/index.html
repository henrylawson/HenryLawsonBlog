Who is Henry Lawson?

Henry is a developer from Brisbane, Australia who is currently living in Chennai, India. He is a passionate agile evangelist and a keen C#, Java, JavaScript and Ruby developer. He struggles to stay on top of all the emerging frameworks and software trends so tries to keep his focus on principles and patterns because he belives that as the frameworks fade, the patterns and principles remain. The writtings here are of his own opinion and should definitely not be taken for granted and should be questioned and scrutinized by you in every way you desire.
